Business Background

Founded in 2006, Manika Jewelry has made supporting local artists a foundational part of its business model. It carries around 20 collections from designers around the world and provides custom services to meet each customers' requirements.

  • Target audience:

    25-45 professional female who like to purchase personalized product to identify their own style.

Persona Creation & Problem Statement

I am a detail oriented person, and I like things to be perfect. I often purchase personalized or customized products to identify my own style.

While I try to customize a jewelry for myself on a website, I can't really imagine the finished product and its texture because it only shows the change of color on the website, and I don't have the knowledge of all the gemstones and metals. This makes me hesitate to shop and create jewelry online.
