  • Gif Animation

Client Definition

Robert Chang, a professional data scientist in weekdays, part-time wedding singer during weekends. As a Taiwanese-American, most of Robert’s clients are Asian couples who require the performance to be singing in both Chinese and English. Since being a wedding singer is his passion and a side job, his price is relatively low while remaining high qualities of performance. He can perform solely with karaoke tapes and has a pianist as his partner and backing vocalist.

He is new to this business but already has a lot of experiences for friends and families. Now he would like to start accepting cases to test the water. Having a brand image and a website would be a good start.

Objective for the Website

Purpose of the website (Call-to-action): Users fill out a form to reach out and ask for a quote.

  • Know better about the wedding singer’s background to have enough confidence.
  • Be able to see some past performances to determine if they like it or not.
  • Find out the basic rates and what to expect.

User Profile

Primary user: Asian young couple who lives in the San Francisco Bay Area or plans to have a wedding in the area.

  • Age: 25 - 35
  • Works in the technology industry. Tech savvy.
  • Accepted a fair amount of American culture but still familiar with Chinese pop culture.
  • Salary above average. Enjoys life but can’t afford to be squandered.
  • Love music. Love partying, and hanging out with friends and family.

Design Concepts

For those potential customers who never know the singer before, the design of the website needs to represent his character and shows a joyful wedding atmosphere. All contents and information will be displayed on a single page website to lead users down to a journey of knowing a little bit better about the wedding singer before taking an action.

  • Logo Design & Typography:

The half-half logo image shows two different identities of the singer: data scientist during weekdays, wedding singer on weekends. A modern, san-serif typeface represents the singer better with a perfect rounded “O” and his last name half across to correspond with the logo image.

  • Color Palette:

The brand color is set to be saturated orange not only because it’s one of the singer’s favorite color, but also because it shows warmth and represents a friendly and joyful brand image. It also serves the purpose to create call to action.
