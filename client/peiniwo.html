Peini.wo: First Personal Website

Personal Project: Oct 2014
  • Brand Identity Design
  • Web Development

Design Concept

As a self-taught designer without related experience before moving to the bay, I believe all experiences matter in order to be a successful designer. I have always felt blessed to have balanced strengths in both sides of the brain. While loving the preciseness of numbers, I also enjoy the inspiration of abstract concepts. Throughout my previous career, I was known as an analytical leader for my ability to collect and analyze raw data, delivering competitive analysis and reports that helps companies make the strategic decisions needed to expand and grow. After moving to the San Francisco Bay Area, I have determined to become a UX designer. It is time to put my right brain in a more importance position.

Under this rationale, when I had to create a personal website, I decided to design a graphic of a left-right brain indicated the ability to be strategic and creative at the same time. The left side of the brain carries my academic background and previous experience, while the right side represents all my trainings and portfolio as a designer.

With the same concept, the logo (same as in this website) has been designed with two different typefaces to demonstrate that a harmonious combination could be possible between a free and unrestrained typeface and a strict one.

As I have learned more about design and coding over the years, I decided to retire the website and updated it to be a trendy portfolio site with responsive design.
