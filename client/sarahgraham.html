Website Assessment

Basically it’s a well-function and ok-designed website. However, there are a lot of event triggers that I would like to redesign and relocate. Moreover, the whole brand image can be improved to be more luxurious and modern.

  • Purpose of the Website:
    • Mainly for brand awareness.
    • E-commerce is not available.
    • Retailers can visit the website and login to obtain information for wholesale.
    • Shoppers can look up the pieces and contact information so that the communication can go smoothly when a purchase is required.

User Profile

  • Prioritize the Audience:
    • Retailer: There are more than 100 retailers in North America carrying the designer’s jewelry.
    • Individual customer: The website offers information about the brand and its jewelry before making a purchase decision.

Design Explorations

The brand has a very unique style which can be distinct from the market. However, the website does not show its unique brand image. The logo is designed with a serif font, which does not match with its modern brand image and website content.

  • Logo Design:

Tried out a few typefaces to give it a free handwritten style to match the designer’s modern, handmade jewelry. Selected a different typeface for the subline to give the entire logo a more stable foundatioin as a whole.

  • Design Iteration:

The original design only focused on cleaning up the redundancy and reorganizing information structure. The creamy background color was kept as it went well with jewelries.

Inspired by the unique design of the jewelry, which only combines with the color of black and yellow, I decided to give the website the same visual design as its company products.
